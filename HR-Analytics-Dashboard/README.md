## Project Overview

This project is an HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI using the IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition dataset.

The objective was to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, compensation patterns, and employee satisfaction factors to identify key drivers of employee turnover.

The dashboard provides HR teams and business leaders with actionable insights to improve employee retention and workforce planning.

---

## Business Problem

Employee attrition can significantly impact productivity, recruitment costs, and organizational performance.

This dashboard was designed to answer key business questions:

* Which departments experience the highest attrition?
* Which job roles are most affected?
* Does overtime influence employee turnover?
* Which age groups are leaving most frequently?
* How does salary affect employee retention?

## Key Insights

* Attrition Rate: 16.12%
* Research & Development experienced the highest employee attrition.
* Laboratory Technicians showed the highest attrition among job roles.
* Employees aged 26–35 represented the highest turnover group.
* Lower salary groups experienced higher attrition rates.
* Employees working overtime were more likely to leave the organization.
* Most employee turnover occurred during the early years of employment.

---

## Business Impact

The dashboard helps HR teams identify high-risk employee groups, understand attrition patterns, and develop targeted retention strategies based on data-driven insights.
