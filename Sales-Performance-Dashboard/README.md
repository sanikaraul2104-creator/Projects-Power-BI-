<img src="Sales Dashboard.png" width="1000">

## Project Overview

This project is a Sales Performance Dashboard built in Power BI using retail sales data.

The objective of the dashboard is to analyze sales, profit, customer segments, and regional performance to help stakeholders understand business trends and make data-driven decisions.

The dashboard provides an interactive view of business performance through KPIs, category analysis, regional insights, and profitability tracking.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often generate large volumes of sales data, making it difficult for decision-makers to quickly identify trends and performance drivers.

This dashboard was designed to answer key business questions:

* Which product categories generate the most sales?
* Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
* Which regions perform best?
* Are there products generating losses despite strong sales?
* What opportunities exist to improve profitability?

## Key Insights

* Technology was the highest-performing product category in terms of sales.
* The Consumer segment contributed the largest share of revenue.
* The West region generated the strongest overall sales performance.
* Certain sub-categories such as Tables and Bookcases generated negative profit despite generating sales revenue.
* Profitability analysis revealed opportunities to optimize pricing and product strategy.

---

## Business Impact

The dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor performance, identify profitable areas, detect loss-generating products, and make informed strategic decisions.
